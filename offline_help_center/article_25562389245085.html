Creating and Downloading Clips, files in Userology

General → Responses and Recordings | Updated: 2025-03-19

This feature allows you to focus on specific parts of your study and share them with others. Alternatively, you can download the full study files consisting of Audio, video and transcript.

1. Accessing the Recordings Section

Navigate to the recordings section to access all sessions related to your study. 2. Identify and select the session for the participant you are interested in. 3. Optionally, choose any other session linked to the same participant for additional review.

 

 

2. Reading the Transcript and Creating a Clip

Locate the complete transcript of the session on the right-hand side. 2. Select the portion of the transcript you wish to highlight and copy it. 3. Click on 'Create a clip'. 4. Assign an appropriate name to your clip, such as 'AI Chatbots'. 5. Your clip will be generated and can be found for download and sharing in the "Clips" tab.

 

 

 

3. Downloading the Clip or the Whole Session

1. Click the 'Download' button to download your newly created clip.

2. Select the desired files to download from - entire video, audio, and/ or transcript.

3. For this example, choose both the video and transcript.

4. Click 'Download' to retrieve your selected files.
